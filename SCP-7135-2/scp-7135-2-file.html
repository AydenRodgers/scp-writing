SCP-7067
rating: +1858+–x

Painting of SCP-7067

Item #: SCP-7067

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7067 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell, measuring at least 5 meters (16 feet) by 5 meters (16 feet) by 3 meters (10 feet) and constructed of reinforced steel and concrete. The containment cell must have no windows, and access to the cell must be controlled through a secure airlock system. SCP-7067 is to be provided with standard amenities for a child, including a bed, a small table, and a chair. The cell must be furnished with child-friendly decor, including posters, toys, and books appropriate for its age. However, all items placed in the containment cell must be pre-approved by at least one Level 3 personnel, and subject to inspection for potential anomalous properties. SCP-7067 is to be monitored by at least two armed guards at all times. These guards must be trained in non-lethal methods of restraint and have experience dealing with anomalous entities with potential telekinetic abilities. At no point should any personnel make physical contact with SCP-7067 without prior approval from at least one Level 3 personnel. SCP-7067's anomalous abilities must be monitored through regular testing, including telekinetic and empathic testing. All testing must be conducted in a controlled environment, and subject to approval by at least one Level 3 personnel. Any anomalies detected during testing must be reported immediately to Site Command. Any personnel entering SCP-7067's containment cell must wear protective gear, including helmets, Kevlar vests, and gloves, to protect against potential telekinetic attacks. In case of emergency, the cell must be equipped with a high-frequency sound system capable of emitting a sound frequency that has been found to neutralize SCP-7067's abilities. In the event of a containment breach, all personnel are instructed to retreat to a safe distance and activate the high-frequency sound system. MTF units trained in dealing with anomalous entities with potential telekinetic abilities are to be dispatched immediately to re-contain SCP-7067.

Description: SCP-7067 is a humanoid child entity that appears to be between 6-8 years old. It has a height of approximately 120 cm (4 feet) and a weight of around 30 kg (66 pounds). SCP-7067 has fair skin and short blonde hair, and its eyes are described as being a bright green color. SCP-7067 is often seen wearing simple clothes, and it does not wear any shoes or socks. It appears to be in a perpetual state of cleanliness, with no visible dirt or grime on its body or clothing. Despite its humanoid appearance, SCP-7067 displays abnormal behavior and abilities, which have led to its classification as an SCP object.

Additional: SCP-7067 was found by The Site Director, Adrian, on April 7th, 2017 in a forest near Site-16

Addendum 7067-01: SCP-7067 shows no affection of that of a male when around SCP-166

Addendum 7067-02: SCP-7067 has facilitated an escape attempt, convincing several of the research staff to aid it in its bid for freedom. Insurrection failed. All staff that have been in contact with SCP-7067 have been terminated, and mandatory psychiatric evaluations have been implemented for all personnel coming in contact with SCP-7067.

Addendum 7067-03: It has been determined that SCP-7067 is capable of telepathy, whether or not it possesses a host, even penetrating to the subconscious of others, and using the knowledge it finds to its advantage. Extreme caution is advised when choosing subjects to converse with SCP-7067.

Addendum 7067-04: SCP-7067 has expressed an interest in other SCPs, most notably SCP-999 and SCP-053. Dr. â–ˆâ–ˆâ–ˆâ–ˆâ–ˆâ–ˆ has expressed worry that should SCP-7067 bond with either, their regenerative qualities would negate its corruption and give it a permanent esteem boost.

Addendum 7067-05: After several more escape attempts, and after reviewing SCP-7067's incident record, high command has ordered that it be permanently sealed within the facility and prohibited from being allowed any more hosts. Several personnel have protested against this, with some even erupting into violence. As a direct result, all personnel that have come into contact with SCP-7067 have been terminated. Going forward, all personnel that deal with SCP-7067 are to be rotated frequently, and contact is to be limited even to its dormant state to as little as possible.

Addendum 7067-06: Personnel within 10 meters of SCP-7067 have recently reported feeling unease, stating that they can hear unintelligible whispering. Several others have suffered from severe migraines. Object has been monitored, but there is no change in its dormant behavior, and no sounds have been recorded.

Addendum 7067-07: SCP-7067 also shows some sort of playfulness with Nu-7(Allowed)

Exploration Video Log Transcript

Date: September 12, 2018

Exploration Team: Epsilon-11

Subject: Light Containment Zone - SCP-7067

Team Lead: N/A

Team Members: Carson Snyder, Nathan James, Liam Burris

-----

[BEGIN LOG]

Dr. James: "Alright Epsilon-11. Don't do anything to get him angered. God knows what he acts like when he is fucking pissed off."

Carson Snyder: "Don't worry boss. We won't anger him badly. We just want to ask him a few questions."

Carson and the others enter the cell, looking for 7067. They find him sitting on a chair, and sat down in front of him

Carson Snyder: "Wake up."

SCP-7067: "Hm?.."

Carson Snyder: "It's interview time 7067."

SCP-7067: "What happened? Why?"

Carson Snyder: "You are a valuable thing to us."

SCP-7067: "I want to go home..."

Carson Snyder: "I'm afraid that's not up to me. But we'll make sure that you're well taken care of here, and we'll do everything we can to help you."

SCP-7067: "Okay..." [SCP-7067 then got down from its chair, and grabbed his teddy bear plush as he headed back to the bed, Dr. REDACTED said 'Good night' and left the containment cell, leaving a flower from SCP-053 on its dresser.]

[END LOG]
